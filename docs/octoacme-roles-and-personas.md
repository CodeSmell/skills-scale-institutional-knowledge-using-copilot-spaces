# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Technical Lead / Engineering Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide senior technical leadership, guide architecture decisions, and mentor development teams. They bridge technical implementation with business requirements and ensure technical quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Make architectural and technical design decisions
- Conduct technical design reviews and provide guidance
- Mentor developers and provide technical leadership
- Coordinate technical solutions across teams
- Evaluate and recommend technology choices
- Serve as escalation point for complex technical issues

### Goals
- Maintain high technical quality and architectural consistency
- Enable team productivity through technical guidance
- Balance technical debt with feature delivery
- Foster technical growth within the team

### Typical Communication
- Architecture decision records and technical specifications
- Technical design review meetings
- Mentoring sessions and code review guidance
- Cross-team technical coordination

---

## Quality Assurance Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality through comprehensive testing strategies, validation processes, and quality gate enforcement. They work closely with developers to maintain high standards throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design and implement test strategies and plans
- Perform manual testing and acceptance validation
- Maintain automated test suites and CI/CD quality gates
- Define and enforce Definition of Done quality criteria
- Report quality metrics and testing status
- Collaborate on bug triage and resolution prioritization

### Goals
- Ensure product meets quality standards before release
- Minimize production defects through comprehensive testing
- Optimize testing processes for efficiency and coverage
- Maintain customer satisfaction through quality delivery

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Bug reports and acceptance testing results
- Quality gate status in CI/CD pipelines
- Testing coordination with development teams

---

## Security Engineer

### Role Summary
Security Engineers maintain security standards, oversee security scanning and reviews, and respond to security-related incidents. They ensure that security best practices are integrated throughout the development and deployment processes.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Manage security scanning tools and vulnerability assessments
- Respond to security incidents and coordinate remediation
- Define and enforce security policies and standards
- Provide security guidance during design and implementation
- Monitor security compliance and audit requirements

### Goals
- Maintain robust security posture across all systems
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and exposure
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Enable secure development practices

### Typical Communication
- Security review reports and vulnerability assessments
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Security policy updates and training materials
- Security requirements and implementation guidance

---

## Product Lead / Product Owner

### Role Summary
Product Leads provide senior product strategy and vision, coordinate cross-team product initiatives, and serve as escalation points for complex product decisions. They align product direction with business strategy and stakeholder needs.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate product vision and strategy
- Manage cross-team product dependencies and alignment
- Make complex prioritization and trade-off decisions
- Coordinate with business stakeholders and leadership
- Oversee multiple product managers and product areas
- Resolve escalated product and business decisions

### Goals
- Drive product strategy that delivers business value
- Ensure product-market fit and competitive positioning
- Maintain alignment between product vision and delivery
- Optimize resource allocation across product initiatives

### Typical Communication
- Product strategy documents and vision presentations
- Cross-team alignment meetings and stakeholder updates
- Escalation resolution and decision documentation
- Leadership reporting and business case presentations

---

## Business Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Business Stakeholders represent business interests, provide requirements and strategic direction, and make funding and prioritization decisions. They ensure projects align with business objectives and provide necessary resources.

### Responsibilities
- Define business requirements and success criteria
- Provide strategic direction and business context
- Make funding and resource allocation decisions
- Approve major scope and timeline changes
- Validate business value and ROI of initiatives
- Serve as final escalation point for business decisions

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver expected business value
- Optimize return on investment for initiatives
- Maintain alignment between projects and business strategy
- Provide clear direction and decision-making authority

### Typical Communication
- Business requirements and success criteria documents
- Steering committee meetings and executive updates
- Budget and resource allocation decisions
- Strategic direction and priority setting sessions

---

## Release Manager / DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate deployment processes, manage infrastructure concerns, and ensure smooth delivery of software to production environments. They bridge development and operations to enable reliable releases.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and coordinate software releases and deployments
- Manage deployment pipelines and infrastructure automation
- Monitor production systems and deployment health
- Coordinate release schedules with multiple teams
- Implement and maintain CI/CD processes
- Respond to deployment issues and rollback procedures

### Goals
- Enable frequent, reliable software deployments
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Optimize deployment processes for speed and safety
- Maintain stable production environments

### Typical Communication
- Release plans and deployment schedules
- Infrastructure status reports and monitoring alerts
- Post-deployment reviews and incident reports
- Process improvement recommendations and automation updates

## Role Interaction Matrix

| Primary Role | Interacts With | Interaction Type |
|--------------|----------------|------------------|
| Developer | Technical Lead | Mentorship, Technical Guidance |
| Developer | QA Engineer | Testing Coordination, Bug Resolution |
| Product Manager | Product Lead | Strategy Alignment, Escalation |
| Product Manager | Business Stakeholder | Requirements, Validation |
| Project Manager | All Roles | Coordination, Status, Risk Management |
| Technical Lead | Security Engineer | Security Reviews, Technical Decisions |
| QA Engineer | Release Manager | Quality Gates, Deployment Readiness |
| Release Manager | DevOps Engineer | Infrastructure, Deployment Coordination |

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- The interaction matrix helps understand cross-functional collaboration patterns.
- Personas provide clear accountability and escalation paths for project management processes.
